Solar Systems

Our smallest standard rooftop solution was installed at a school in Gurgaon to comply with the requirement to generate 5% of their power usage through solar. The system has been installed with a plan for future expansion as the power needs of the school increase. A Net Metering system is being used at the site to ensure the school receives credit for power generated through solar during non working days such as Sundays and school holidays

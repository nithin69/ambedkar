About Us

National NDT Services (NNDTS) Profile :

National NDT Services (NNDTS) was established in Visakhapatnam (INDIA) and is An ISO 9001:2015 (Quality Management Systems) & ISO 10002:2014 (Customer Satisfaction and Complaint Management System) Certified Organization. We have registered organization in Small Scale Industries (SSI) Govt. Of Andhraparadesh (INDIA).We have a Association member in National Level Life time Membership in Association of NDT Services Organization in INDIA called as NANSO. We are a Non Destructive Testing services one of the leading NDT Services as Testing & Inspection, NDT Training institutes in INDIA.

National NDT Services (NNDTS) provide all the High Technology Inspection Services & Training Courses in Non Destructive Testing (NDT) Methods of Radiography Testing (RT), Ultrasonic Testing (UT), Dye Penetrating Testing (DPT), Magnetic Particle Testing (MPT), Visual Testing (VT), Post Welding Heat Treatment (PWHT), Ultrasonic Thickness Gauging (UTG), Positive Material Identification (PMI), Hardness Testing (HT), NDT level I & Level II & Level III training and Trained NDT Manpower provider etc. Our inspection Staff having experience above 10 Years in above mentioned methods.

We assured to - Candidates will receive training from experienced Faculty with Theory & Practical’s. Our Faculty having a minimum above 13 years of experience in the industry. We providing Training in NDT ASNT Level I & Level II methods with certification as per SNT-TC-1A, Of American Society of Non-Destructive Testing (ASNT,USA) Board requirement.

National NDT Services (NNDTS) offers a diverse range of Quality Control (QC) NDT Inspection Courses & Testing Services for Customers and Students in the field of Oil & Gas, Refiners, Petro Chemical, Energy Plants, Shipping’s, Power Boilers, Fabrication & Engineering and Constructions Industrial Sectors.

We have one more Organization name Of HR Consultants International. We are the Manpower Supplier from INDIA. We are providing all types of manpower recruitment services and having a strong history of top line manpower solutions professional, executive level, skilled, semi-skilled and unskilled workforce from INDIA.
